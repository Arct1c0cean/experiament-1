2000's Online Games

I grew up on late 2000's online games, and all of them bring back so many memories. Most of them were worlds you could explore, and you could minimally interact with others around you. For some reason a lot of these games tailored to young boys, but younger me growing up with brothers found myself playing them more than other online games. All of them feature eye catching bright color pallets, rounded shapes, and cartoony mascots for players to customize. It was the earlist form of online avatar creation I had.

  • Poptropica
  • Club Penguin
  • Webkinz
